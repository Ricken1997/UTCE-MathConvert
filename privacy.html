Privacy Policy for UTCE MathConvert

UTCE MathConvert ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Diagnostic information
  • Advertising identifiers

Mathematical Expressions

Mathematical expressions entered into the App are processed locally on your Mac.

They are not transmitted to any server, stored online, or shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising SDKs
  • Tracking technologies
  • Cloud processing services

Data Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no user data stored by the App.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children or any other users.
