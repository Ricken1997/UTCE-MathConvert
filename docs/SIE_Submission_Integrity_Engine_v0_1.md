Submission Integrity Engine (SIE) v0.1

Position

Submission Integrity Engine (SIE) is a subsystem of the Academic Intelligence Engine.

Its purpose is not manuscript writing itself, but maintaining the integrity of submission activities.

⸻

Core Mission

Prevent:

* duplicate submissions
* version confusion
* title confusion
* accidental resubmission
* loss of review history
* ethical problems caused by human memory limitations

Significance

Submission failures are often not failures of academic strategy.

They are failures of information management.

Therefore, maintaining manuscript identity and submission integrity requires software support rather than human memory.

Submission Integrity Engine (SIE) is intended to provide that support.

This module constitutes one layer of the broader Academic Intelligence Engine.
